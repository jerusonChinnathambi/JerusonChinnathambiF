01

Executive summary

JRSN MAX TUNE is an AI-powered vocal production tool that pairs real-time pitch correction and DSP processing with a conversational AI assistant. As Product Manager and Product Owner, I took it from concept to implementation — identifying the user pain point, defining the vision, prioritizing the MVP, designing the AI workflow, and driving the roadmap from a custom DSP engine to an intelligent, AI-assisted production environment.

The core idea: let creators describe the sound they want in plain language while the AI builds a professional vocal chain — without ever taking away manual control.

02

The business problem

Problem
Professional vocal plugins expose dozens of technical controls — Auto-Tune, EQ, compression, saturation, stereo imaging, delay, reverb, modulation. Producers burn time on setup before any creative work begins, and beginners face a wall of jargon that takes years to learn.
The opportunity
Remove the technical friction: let users say what they want — “make this hook sound expensive,” “make this vocal wider and emotional” — and have AI assemble an optimized chain they can still fine-tune by hand.
03

The product

A Y2K-inspired interface with a real-time pitch engine, a full effects chain, and an AI Mix Assistant that turns prompts into production decisions.

Real-time pitch correction. Detected vs. corrected pitch, retune speed, humanize, formant, and preset voicings — built on a custom C++ DSP engine.

AI Mix Assistant. Describe the vocal in plain language; the AI generates a full chain. Every parameter stays editable, with prompt history and favorites.

04

Product strategy

Four principles kept the product useful to pros and approachable for beginners.

01 AI-ASSISTED CREATIVITY

Speak, don't engineer

Creators communicate in natural language instead of DSP terminology.

02 MANUAL CONTROL

Nothing is locked

Every AI-generated parameter remains fully editable — the AI assists, it never overrides the creator.

03 REAL-TIME

Low-latency by design

AI makes production decisions while a deterministic DSP engine handles real-time audio.

04 CREATOR-CENTRIC UX

Transparent & flexible

Reduce complexity while keeping the workflow explainable and the creator in charge.

05

How it works

Cloud-based generative AI plans the chain; the local DSP engine performs the audio. That split delivers low latency, explainable behavior, and full manual editing.

Prompt-to-audio workflowAI plans · DSP performs

A plain-language prompt becomes structured parameters, mapped into the real-time DSP engine — then the creator fine-tunes by hand.
